## 1. Professional Onboarding & Vetting

### 1.1 Application Process

**Step 1: Initial Application (Online)**
- Professional submits application form with:
  - Full name, ID number, contact information
  - Services offered and experience level
  - Availability and preferred work areas
  - Rate expectations
  - Two professional references
  - Work permit (if applicable for foreign professionals)

**Step 2: Document Collection**
- Government-issued ID (Cédula de Ciudadanía or Cédula de Extranjería)
- Proof of address (utility bill, lease agreement)
- Reference contact information (minimum 2, preferably 3)
- Professional certifications (if applicable)

**Step 3: Background Verification (3-5 business days)**
- Identity verification through Registraduría Nacional
- Criminal background check (Certificado de Antecedentes Penales)
- Reference checks (phone interviews)
- Address verification
- Social media presence review

**Decision Criteria:**
- ✅ Clear criminal record
- ✅ Positive references (minimum 2 of 3)
- ✅ Valid identification documents
- ✅ Minimum 1 year relevant experience OR professional certification
- ❌ Any felony convictions
- ❌ Fraud or theft history
- ❌ Unable to verify identity or references

### 1.2 In-Person Interview & Assessment

**Location**: Office or neutral public location (café, co-working space)

**Interview Checklist:**
- [ ] Verify identity documents against originals
- [ ] Assess Spanish/English language proficiency
- [ ] Review experience and service capabilities
- [ ] Discuss expectations, rates, and platform policies
- [ ] Assess professionalism and communication skills
- [ ] Explain mutual rating system and its importance
- [ ] Review safety protocols and emergency procedures

**Red Flags:**
- Inconsistent stories about experience
- Unable to provide specific examples of past work
- Aggressive or unprofessional demeanor
- Unrealistic rate expectations (too high or suspiciously low)

### 1.3 Profile Creation

**Professional Photography Session:**
- Schedule professional photo shoot (cost covered by platform)
- 2-3 professional headshots
- Action shots in appropriate work attire (optional)
- Photos reviewed for professionalism before publishing

**Profile Content:**
- Bio (150-300 words) - drafted with professional, translated if needed
- Services list with detailed descriptions
- Hourly/daily rates
- Availability calendar
- Languages spoken
- Special skills or certifications

**Approval Timeline:** Profile goes live within 24 hours of photo shoot completion

### 1.4 Probationary Period

**First 30 Days or 5 Bookings (whichever comes first):**
- Close monitoring of ratings and reviews
- Customer satisfaction follow-up after each booking
- Platform representative check-in at day 15
- Minimum 4.0 average rating required to continue
- Below 4.0: Performance review and potential additional training or removal

## 2. Customer Onboarding & Verification

### 2.1 Registration Process

**Required Information:**
- Full name and email
- Phone number (with WhatsApp if available)
- Location (city and neighborhood)
- Property type (apartment, house, office)
- Preferred language

**Identity Verification:**
- **Tier 1 (Basic)**: Email and phone verification
- **Tier 2 (Standard)**: Upload government ID or passport
- **Tier 3 (Enhanced)**: Video verification call with support team

*Note: Professionals can set minimum verification tier for their bookings*

### 2.2 First Booking Requirements

Before first booking:
- Complete profile with photo (optional but recommended)
- Verify payment method
- Accept terms of service and community guidelines
- Watch 2-minute safety video (covers mutual respect, property preparation, emergency contacts)

### 2.3 Customer Profile & Ratings

**Customer Ratings Visible to Professionals:**
- Overall rating from past professionals
- Number of completed bookings
- Verification level badge
- Reviews from professionals (if any)

**Rating System for Customers:**
- Respectfulness and communication
- Property condition and safety
- Payment promptness
- Clarity of instructions

## 3. Booking Management

### 3.1 Booking Flow

**Customer Initiates:**
1. Search/filter for professionals
2. Select professional and desired service
3. Choose date, time, and duration
4. Add special instructions (pets, access codes, specific tasks)
5. Confirm booking with payment authorization

**Professional Receives:**
- Instant notification (push + email + SMS)
- 24 hours to accept or decline
- View customer profile and rating
- Option to ask questions via messaging before accepting

**Booking Confirmation:**
- Both parties receive confirmation with details
- Calendar invites sent
- 24-hour and 2-hour reminder notifications
- Access to in-app messaging

### 3.2 Booking Modifications

**Rescheduling by Customer:**
- Free cancellation/reschedule up to 24 hours before
- 50% charge for 12-24 hour notice
- Full charge for <12 hour notice or no-show

**Rescheduling by Professional:**
- Must provide 48-hour notice
- If less than 48 hours: Customer receives 20% discount on next booking
- If no-show: Professional suspended pending review

**Emergency Cancellations:**
- Medical emergencies, family emergencies, natural disasters
- Must provide documentation
- No penalty with valid documentation

### 3.3 Day-of-Service Protocol

**Professional Check-in (Required):**
- Arrive within 15-minute window of scheduled time
- Check-in via app upon arrival (GPS verified)
- Confirm service scope with customer
- Note any special conditions or changes

**During Service:**
- Access to emergency support line
- Ability to extend time (with customer approval via app)
- Document any issues or damages found

**Professional Check-out (Required):**
- Check-out via app upon completion
- Request rating and review from customer
- Note any additional services performed

## 4. Quality Assurance & Ratings

### 4.1 Rating System Mechanics

**Timing:**
- Rating request sent immediately after service completion
- 48-hour window to submit rating
- Both parties must rate before seeing the other's rating

**Rating Categories (1-5 stars):**

*For Professionals:*
- Quality of work
- Punctuality
- Professionalism
- Communication
- Overall experience

*For Customers:*
- Respectfulness
- Communication clarity
- Property condition
- Payment promptness
- Overall experience

### 4.2 Review Guidelines

**Acceptable Reviews:**
- Factual descriptions of service experience
- Constructive feedback
- Specific examples of exceptional or poor service

**Prohibited Content:**
- Personal attacks or discriminatory language
- Threats or harassment
- False information
- Irrelevant content
- Disclosure of personal information (addresses, phone numbers)

**Review Moderation:**
- All reviews screened within 24 hours
- Flagged reviews investigated within 48 hours
- Users can dispute reviews with evidence

### 4.3 Quality Monitoring

**Professional Performance Tracking:**
- Weekly performance dashboard sent to professionals
- Average rating alerts if below 4.3
- Automatic review if rating drops below 4.0 for 3+ consecutive bookings

**Customer Performance Tracking:**
- Customers with <3.5 rating receive guidance on platform etiquette
- Customers with <3.0 rating may have booking privileges suspended

**High Performer Recognition:**
- "Top Professional" badge for 4.8+ rating with 20+ bookings
- Featured placement in search results
- Access to premium customers
- Invitation to exclusive platform events

## 5. Dispute Resolution

### 5.1 Dispute Categories

**Type A: Service Quality Issues**
- Work not completed as agreed
- Professional arrived late or left early
- Poor quality results

**Type B: Property or Safety Issues**
- Damage to property
- Theft or missing items
- Safety concerns or inappropriate behavior

**Type C: Payment Disputes**
- Overcharging
- Payment processing errors
- Refund requests

**Type D: Behavioral Issues**
- Disrespectful communication
- Harassment
- Violation of community guidelines

### 5.2 Resolution Process

**Step 1: Initial Report (Day 0)**
- User files dispute via app with category selection
- Provides description and evidence (photos, messages, receipts)
- Ticket automatically created and assigned to support agent

**Step 2: Initial Contact (Within 4 hours)**
- Support agent contacts reporting party
- Gathers additional information
- Contacts other party for their perspective
- Documents both accounts

**Step 3: Investigation (1-3 business days)**
- Review all evidence
- Check platform data (GPS check-ins, messages, booking history)
- Consult with other party
- Review user history for patterns

**Step 4: Resolution Determination**

*For Service Quality Issues:*
- Partial refund (25-100% depending on severity)
- Free rebooking with different professional
- Professional receives warning or additional training

*For Property Damage/Theft:*
- Insurance claim filed if > $100 USD equivalent
- Police report required for theft claims
- Professional suspended pending investigation
- Permanent ban if confirmed

*For Payment Disputes:*
- Transaction review and adjustment
- Refund processed within 5 business days

*For Behavioral Issues:*
- Warning for first offense (if minor)
- Temporary suspension (7-30 days) for serious offenses
- Permanent ban for harassment, discrimination, or threats

**Step 5: Follow-up (7 days post-resolution)**
- Check-in with both parties
- Confirm satisfaction with resolution
- Gather feedback on dispute process

### 5.3 Escalation Path

**Level 1**: Customer Support Agent (handles 80% of disputes)
**Level 2**: Operations Manager (complex cases, repeat offenders)
**Level 3**: Legal Team (potential legal matters, serious safety issues)

## 6. Payment Processing

### 6.1 Payment Methods

**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- Colombian bank transfers (PSE)
- Digital wallets (Nequi, Daviplata)
- International cards (for foreign customers)

### 6.2 Payment Flow

**Booking Payment:**
1. Customer's payment method authorized at booking (not charged)
2. Payment held in escrow
3. Payment captured 2 hours after service completion
4. Platform commission deducted (18%)
5. Professional receives payout within 48 hours

**Payout Schedule:**
- Twice weekly (Tuesdays and Fridays)
- Minimum payout threshold: $50,000 COP (~$12 USD)
- Bank transfer to Colombian account or international transfer

### 6.3 Pricing Guidelines

**Platform Recommendations:**
- Cleaning services: $15,000-$30,000 COP/hour
- Deep cleaning: $25,000-$45,000 COP/hour
- Cooking services: $20,000-$40,000 COP/hour
- Laundry & ironing: $15,000-$25,000 COP/hour

*Professionals set their own rates within or outside these ranges*

### 6.4 Tipping

- Optional tip function (10%, 15%, 20%, or custom)
- 100% of tips go to professional
- Tip can be added up to 7 days after service

### 7.1 Support Channels

**Available Channels:**
- In-app chat (24/7 for urgent issues, 8am-8pm for general inquiries)
- Email: support@[platform].com (response within 24 hours)
- WhatsApp: +57 [number] (urgent issues only)
- Emergency hotline: +57 [number] (safety emergencies only)

### 7.2 Response Time SLAs

**Critical Issues** (safety, emergency): 15 minutes
**Urgent** (booking issues day-of): 1 hour
**High Priority** (payment issues, disputes): 4 hours
**Medium** (profile updates, general questions): 24 hours
**Low** (feature requests, feedback): 48 hours

### 7.3 Common Support Scenarios

**Scenario: Professional No-Show**
1. Confirm no-show with customer
2. Attempt to contact professional (call + message)
3. If no response within 30 minutes:
   - Cancel booking and refund customer
   - Apply $20 USD credit to customer account
   - Mark professional for review
4. Follow up with professional to understand issue

**Scenario: Customer Not Home at Scheduled Time**
1. Professional confirms via app
2. Professional waits 15 minutes, attempts to contact customer
3. If no response:
   - Professional can leave and gets paid 50% of booking
   - Customer charged cancellation fee
4. Support team contacts customer to understand issue

**Scenario: Scope Creep (Customer requests extra work)**
1. Professional should use in-app messaging to request booking extension
2. Customer approves extension with adjusted price
3. If customer refuses to pay for extra time, professional can decline additional work
4. Support mediates if needed

### 7.4 Support Team Training

**Required Training Modules:**
- Platform policies and procedures (8 hours)
- Dispute resolution and de-escalation (4 hours)
- Cultural sensitivity training (Colombian and expat perspectives) (3 hours)
- Payment systems and troubleshooting (2 hours)
- Safety and emergency protocols (2 hours)
- Spanish/English bilingual communication (ongoing)

**Performance Metrics:**
- Customer satisfaction score (CSAT) target: >4.5/5
- First response time: 90% within SLA
- Resolution rate: 85% resolved on first contact
- Monthly ticket volume per agent: 150-200

## 8. Safety & Emergency Protocols

### 8.1 Pre-Service Safety Measures

**For Professionals:**
- Share booking details with trusted contact
- Verify customer profile and rating before accepting
- Trust your instincts - decline bookings that feel unsafe
- Arrive and leave during daylight hours when possible (first-time customers)

**For Customers:**
- Secure valuables before professional arrives
- Provide clear access instructions
- Be present for first booking with new professional (recommended)
- Keep emergency contacts readily available

### 8.2 During-Service Safety

**Both Parties Should:**
- Keep communication professional and respectful
- Use in-app messaging for all service-related communication
- Report any uncomfortable situations immediately

**Red Flags to Report:**
- Requests to go "off-platform" for payment
- Inappropriate comments or behavior
- Feeling unsafe or threatened
- Discovery of illegal activity

### 8.3 Emergency Response Protocol

**Level 1 Emergency: Safety Threat or Medical Emergency**

*Immediate Actions:*
1. Call local emergency services (123 in Colombia)
2. Call platform emergency hotline
3. Remove yourself from dangerous situation if possible

*Platform Response:*
- Support agent dispatched immediately
- Contact authorities if needed
- Document incident thoroughly
- Suspend involved accounts pending investigation
- Coordinate with local authorities
- Provide support resources (counseling, legal assistance if needed)

**Level 2 Emergency: Property Damage or Accident**

*Immediate Actions:*
1. Document with photos/video
2. Report via app immediately
3. Do not attempt to resolve without platform involvement

*Platform Response:*
- Insurance claim initiated within 4 hours
- Investigation launched
- Temporary service suspension if needed
- Resolution within 5 business days

**Level 3 Emergency: Severe Weather or Civil Unrest**

*Platform Actions:*
- Mass communication to all users in affected area
- Automatic cancellation of bookings with full refunds
- No penalties applied to either party
- Regular updates via app, email, SMS

### 8.4 Insurance Coverage

**Professional Liability Insurance:**
- Coverage: Up to $10,000 USD per incident
- Covers: Property damage, personal injury during service
- Professional contributes: $5 USD/month via platform fee

**Platform Insurance:**
- General liability coverage: $1,000,000 USD
- Cyber liability: $500,000 USD
- Covers: Data breaches, platform-related incidents

### 8.5 Background Check Renewal

**Annual Requirements:**
- Criminal background check renewal (every 12 months)
- Reference re-verification (every 24 months)
- Profile review and update
- Safety training refresher

**Failure to Renew:**
- 30-day warning before expiration
- Profile deactivated if not renewed within 30 days
- Can be reactivated upon completion
