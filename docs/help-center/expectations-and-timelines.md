## What If My Request Is Urgent?

### For Immediate Needs (Same-Day Service)

If you need a professional **today** or **within 48 hours**:

1. **Use Professional Search** instead of submitting a brief
   - Filter by "Available Now" or "Available This Week"
   - Book directly with instant confirmation
   - Many professionals offer same-day or next-day service

2. **Indicate Urgency** in your brief
   - Mark your request as "Urgent" or "Immediate"
   - Specify your deadline clearly
   - We'll prioritize your request

3. **Contact Concierge Directly**
   - Use the Amara chat widget
   - Call our support line (emergencies only)
   - Explain your urgent need

**Note:** Urgent requests may have limited professional availability and premium pricing.

## Common Questions

### Why does custom matching take 5 business days?

We manually review every request and handpick professionals based on:
- Your specific requirements and preferences
- Professional availability and location
- Service history and performance ratings
- Background check status and references
- Cultural fit and household compatibility

**This careful process ensures you get the right match, not just any match.**

### Can I get matches faster?

Yes! Options for faster service:
- **Use self-service booking** - Browse and book professionals instantly
- **Set lower matching criteria** - Fewer requirements = faster matches
- **Be flexible on timing** - More flexible schedules = more professional availability

### What if I'm not happy with the matches?

No problem! We offer:
- **Free re-matching** - We'll find new professionals at no extra cost
- **Refined preferences** - Tell us what didn't work, and we'll adjust
- **Unlimited revisions** - We keep searching until you're satisfied
