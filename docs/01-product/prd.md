# Product Requirements Document: Trusted Home Services Marketplace

## Overview
A two-sided marketplace connecting foreigners in Colombia with pre-vetted domestic home service professionals through verified profiles, mutual ratings, and transparent pricing.

## Problem Statement
Foreigners in Colombia struggle to find trustworthy domestic home services (maids, cleaners, maintenance workers) due to language barriers, lack of local networks, and trust concerns about property safety. Simultaneously, qualified service professionals lack access to clients who value and respect their work.

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Foreigners living in or relocating to Colombia seeking domestic home services
- **Secondary**: Pre-screened Colombian home service professionals seeking reliable clientele

## Core Value Proposition
**For Customers**: Hire verified home service professionals with confidence through transparent profiles, ratings, and vetted backgrounds.

**For Professionals**: Access to respectful clients, fair compensation, and reputation-building through mutual ratings.

## Key Features

### Professional Profiles
- Professional photography and personal introduction
- Background verification badge
- Services offered (cleaning, cooking, laundry, organization, etc.)
- Hourly/daily rates and payment methods
- Real-time availability calendar
- Cumulative rating and review history
- Languages spoken
- Years of experience and specializations

### Customer Profiles
- Basic identity verification
- Rating and review history from professionals
- Property type and location (general area)
- Preferred communication language
- Booking history

### Booking System
- Search and filter by service type, location, availability, and price
- Direct booking with calendar integration
- Booking confirmation and reminders
- In-app messaging for coordination

### Mutual Rating System
- Post-service rating (1-5 stars) from both parties
- Written reviews (optional)
- Response mechanism for disputed reviews
- Rating impacts visibility in search results

### Trust & Safety
- ID verification for both professionals and customers
- Background checks for all service professionals
- Secure payment processing
- Emergency contact system
- Report/block functionality

## Success Metrics
- Number of active service professionals on platform
- Monthly bookings completed
- Average professional rating (target: >4.5/5)
- Average customer rating (target: >4.5/5)
- Repeat booking rate
- Time-to-first-booking for new users

## Revenue Model
- Commission on completed bookings (15-20%)
- Optional premium listings for professionals
- Background check fee (one-time for professionals)

## Launch Strategy
**Phase 1**: Bogotá and Medellín with 50 vetted professionals across cleaning and cooking services

**Phase 2**: Expand to Cartagena, Cali; add maintenance and childcare services

**Phase 3**: Scale to additional cities and specialized services

## Technical Requirements
- Web application (responsive mobile design)
- Native mobile apps (iOS/Android) in Phase 2
- Spanish/English bilingual interface
- Payment gateway integration (local Colombian methods + international cards)
- Calendar/scheduling system
- Photo upload and storage
- Push notifications
- Geolocation services

## Key Risks & Mitigation
**Risk**: Professional no-shows or poor service quality  
**Mitigation**: Deposit system, insurance requirements, probationary rating period

**Risk**: Customer safety concerns or property damage  
**Mitigation**: Insurance verification, clear cancellation policies, dispute resolution process

**Risk**: Low initial supply of professionals  
**Mitigation**: Referral bonuses, grassroots recruitment in expat communities

## Go-to-Market Approach
- Partner with expat communities, digital nomad groups, and relocation services
- Social media presence in expat Facebook groups and forums
- Referral incentives for both customers and professionals
- Content marketing about living in Colombia as a foreigner
