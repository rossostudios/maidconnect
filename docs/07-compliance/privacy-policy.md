1. **Right of Access** - Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Right to Rectification** - Correct inaccurate data
3. **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")** - Delete your data
4. **Right to Restriction** - Limit how we use your data
5. **Right to Data Portability** - Receive your data in a machine-readable format
6. **Right to Object** - Object to processing based on legitimate interest
7. **Right to Withdraw Consent** - Withdraw consent for marketing/optional features
8. **Right to Lodge a Complaint** - File a complaint with supervisory authority

### Colombian Law 1581 Rights

Under Law 1581, you have the right to:

1. **Conocer (Know)** - Access your data and how it's used
2. **Actualizar (Update)** - Correct outdated or inaccurate data
3. **Rectificar (Rectify)** - Correct errors in your data
4. **Suprimir (Delete)** - Request deletion when legally permissible
5. **Revocar Autorización (Revoke Consent)** - Withdraw consent (subject to contractual obligations)

**Important Limitations:**
- We may retain data required for legal compliance (tax records, etc.)
- We cannot delete data necessary for active service contracts
- Some data must be retained for dispute resolution (6 months post-booking)

## Colombian Law 1581 Specific Provisions

### Authorization for Data Processing

**Explicit Consent Required:** In compliance with Article 9 of Law 1581, we obtain your express, prior, and informed consent before processing your personal data.

**Consent Mechanism:**
- During account registration, you must accept this Privacy Policy
- Checkbox confirmation for data processing
- Optional consents for marketing communications
- Separate consent for sensitive data (if applicable)

**What You Authorize:**
By creating a MaidConnect account, you authorize us to:
1. Collect and process the personal data described in this policy
2. Transfer your data to third-party processors (listed above)
3. Transfer your data internationally (with adequate protections)
4. Use your data for the purposes described in this policy

### Data Subject Rights (Law 1581)

Per Article 8 of Law 1581, you have the right to:

1. **Access your data** - Free of charge, once per month
2. **Update inaccurate data** - Correct errors or outdated information
3. **Request deletion** - When data is no longer necessary or consent is withdrawn
4. **Revoke consent** - Subject to legal or contractual obligations
5. **File complaints** - With the SIC if we violate your rights

**Exercise Procedure:**
1. Email privacy@casaora.co with subject "Derecho de Habeas Data"
2. Include: Full name, ID number, specific request, contact details
3. We respond within 15 business days (per Decree 1377 of 2013)
4. If denied, you may file a complaint with SIC

### Sensitive Data (Datos Sensibles)

**Definition (Article 5, Law 1581):** Data affecting intimacy or whose misuse could lead to discrimination.

**Our Handling:**
- We do NOT collect sensitive data (religious beliefs, political opinions, health data, biometric data) as part of standard operations
- If sensitive data is voluntarily provided (e.g., in support messages), we treat it with heightened security
- Sensitive data requires explicit, separate consent

### Children's Data (Under 18)

**Prohibited:** Per Article 7 of Law 1581, we do NOT process data of minors without parental consent. Our platform is restricted to users 18+.

### Data Security Obligation (Article 17)

We comply with Article 17 of Law 1581 by implementing:
- Technical safeguards (encryption, access controls)
- Administrative safeguards (employee training, DPAs)
- Physical safeguards (secure data centers via Supabase/Vercel)

### Data Retention (Articles 11-12)

We retain data only as long as necessary for:
- Fulfilling the purpose for which it was collected
- Complying with legal obligations (tax, commercial code)
- Defending legal claims

**Deletion:** Data is deleted when no longer necessary, unless legally required to retain (e.g., tax records).
