# Casaora Business Plan: Monetization Strategy
## Executive Summary

**Date:** November 2025
**Version:** 1.0
**Prepared by:** CEO, Casaora

---

## Company Overview

Casaora is a concierge-style marketplace connecting foreigners living in Colombia with vetted, trusted domestic service professionals. We solve a critical pain point: language barriers, trust issues, and quality uncertainty when hiring household help in a new country. Our platform provides peace of mind through rigorous vetting, transparent pricing, insurance coverage, and bilingual support.

---

## Market Opportunity

### Target Market Sizing
- **Primary Market:** 100,000+ foreign residents in Colombia (digital nomads, expats, retirees)
- **Secondary Market:** 2M+ middle-to-upper class Colombian households seeking premium services
- **Serviceable Market:** $450M+ annual household services spend in major Colombian cities
- **Growth Drivers:** Remote work trends, Colombia's digital nomad visa program, rising middle class

### Target Customer Segments
1. **Digital Nomads & Expats (60% of revenue):** Ages 25-45, tech workers, entrepreneurs, earning $60K+ USD annually
2. **Retiree Expats (25% of revenue):** Ages 55+, seeking reliable long-term domestic help
3. **Affluent Colombians (15% of revenue):** Premium tier seeking vetted, insured professionals

---

## Unique Value Proposition

### For Customers:
- **Trust & Safety:** Background checks, insurance coverage, verified reviews
- **Language Bridge:** Bilingual platform and customer support (English/Spanish)
- **Convenience:** One-click booking, transparent pricing, guaranteed quality
- **Concierge Experience:** White-glove service vs. generic gig marketplace

### For Service Professionals:
- **Steady Income:** Access to high-paying clientele willing to pay premium rates
- **Professional Development:** Training programs, certification badges, career growth
- **Financial Inclusion:** Guaranteed payments, digital wallet, access to benefits

## Revenue Model

### Primary Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction Fees (70% of revenue)**
   - Service commission: 18-22% on all bookings
   - Tiered structure: Higher margin on one-time services (25%), lower on recurring (15%)
   - Target: $12M ARR by Year 3

2. **Subscription Plans (20% of revenue)**
   - **Casaora Plus** (for customers): $19/month - priority booking, discounts, concierge support
   - **Professional Pro** (for service providers): $29/month - premium profile, lead prioritization
   - Target: 8,000 subscribers by Year 3 = $3.4M ARR

3. **Value-Added Services (10% of revenue)**
   - Insurance add-ons: Theft protection, damage coverage (+$5-15 per booking)
   - Express booking fee: Priority scheduling (+$10-20)
   - Background check fees for professionals (one-time: $35)
   - Target: $1.7M ARR by Year 3

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors
- **Generic Marketplaces:** Rappi, Uber-style services - lack specialization and trust layer
- **Traditional Agencies:** Offline, expensive, limited digital experience
- **Facebook Groups:** Unvetted, no insurance, unreliable

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Niche Focus:** Purpose-built for foreigners vs. generic local services
2. **Trust Infrastructure:** Background checks, insurance, verified reviews
3. **Bilingual Concierge:** Human touch + technology
4. **Network Effects:** More professionals → better matches → more customers → attracts more professionals

### Defensibility
- Strong brand trust with target demographic
- Data moat: proprietary matching algorithm improves with scale
- Switching costs: Customer relationship history, saved preferences, recurring bookings
- First-mover advantage in underserved expat segment

## Growth Strategy

### Phase 1: Market Penetration (Year 1)
- **Focus:** Bogotá and Medellín (80% of target market)
- **Customer Acquisition:** Content marketing, expat community partnerships, referral program
- **Key Metrics:** 2,500 customers, 200 professionals, $500K revenue

### Phase 2: Market Expansion (Year 2)
- **Geographic:** Launch in Cartagena, Cali, and coastal regions
- **Vertical:** Add premium services (personal chefs, childcare, elderly care)
- **Technology:** AI-powered matching, automated scheduling optimization
- **Key Metrics:** 8,000 customers, 600 professionals, $2M revenue

### Phase 3: Scale & Diversification (Year 3)
- **Regional Expansion:** Panama, Costa Rica, Mexico (similar expat markets)
- **B2B Channel:** Corporate packages for relocation companies, coworking spaces
- **Platform Features:** Marketplace for home supplies, professional training academy
- **Key Metrics:** 20,000 customers, 1,500 professionals, $5.5M revenue

## Risk Mitigation

### Operational Risks
- **Quality Control:** Implement tiered vetting (basic, standard, premium), mystery shopper program
- **Supply Shortages:** Professional recruitment team, retention bonuses, referral incentives
- **Safety Incidents:** $2M liability insurance, 24/7 incident response team, transparent reporting

### Market Risks
- **Competition:** Build strong brand moat, focus on superior customer experience, retain top professionals exclusively
- **Economic Downturn:** Diversify into essential services (cleaning, childcare), offer flexible pricing tiers
- **Regulatory Changes:** Proactive compliance team, partnerships with labor organizations, transparent contractor model

### Financial Risks
- **Cash Flow:** Maintain 12-month runway, milestone-based fundraising, achieve profitability by Month 18
- **Unit Economics:** Target 60% gross margin, CAC payback < 6 months, LTV:CAC ratio > 3:1
- **Currency Volatility:** Dual pricing (USD/COP), hedging strategies for cross-border transactions

## Key Performance Indicators

### North Star Metrics
- **Gross Merchandise Value (GMV):** Total booking value processed
- **Net Revenue:** After refunds and cancellations
- **Active Users:** Monthly transacting customers and professionals

- **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** $1,200 (24 months avg. retention)
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** $60 (blended across channels)
- **Professional Acquisition Cost:** $150 (includes vetting + onboarding)
- **Contribution Margin:** 65% per transaction

### Operational Excellence
- **Booking Completion Rate:** >92%
- **Customer Retention:** >60% at 12 months
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** >70
- **Professional Utilization:** >75% capacity
